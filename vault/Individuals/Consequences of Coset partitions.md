> [!Theorem]+ Thm: [[Consequences of Coset partitions]]
> Let $G$ be a group, $H$ be a subgroup of $G$ and let $a,b\in G$. Then
> - $aH=bH$ if and only if $b^{-1}a\in H$
> - $eH=H$
> - $aH=H$ if and only if $a\in H$
> - if $a\in bH$ then $aH=bH$
