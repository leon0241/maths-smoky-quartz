> [!Theorem]+ Thm: [[Mean Value Theorem]]
> If $f:[a,b]\to\R$, where $a<b$ is continuous in $[a,b]$ and differentiable in $(a,b)$ then there exists $c\in (a,b)$ such that
> $$f'(c)=\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}$$
