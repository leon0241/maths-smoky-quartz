> [!Theorem]+ Thm: [[Rolle's Theorem]]
> Let $a,b\in \R,\,a<b$. If $f:[a,b] \to \R$ is continuous in $[a,b]$, differentiable in $(a,b)$ and $f(a)=f(b)$, then there exists a point $c$ in $(a,b)$ such that $f'(c)=0$
