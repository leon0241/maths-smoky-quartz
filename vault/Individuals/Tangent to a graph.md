> [!Definition]+ Def: [[Tangent to a graph]]
> If $f$ is differentiable at $x_{0}$ then the straight line given by
> $$y=f(x_{0}) + f'(x_{0})(x-x_{0})$$
> is called the tangent to the graph of $f$ at $(x_{0},f(x_{0}))$. $f'(x_{0})$ is the slope.
